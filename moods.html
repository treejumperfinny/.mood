.mood

Hi, there! How are you today?

Welcome back to your daily mood tracker. We hope you have had a great day, good moment, or not! That's completly fine too. Emotions are never what we expect them to be, and life is full of different kinds of emotions. Never be afraid to feel.



Words to live by. Or not...

Let's take a quick assessment?

Remind yourself to practice a tiny bit of mindfulness. Reflect upon what you did today and how you feel. If you want to skip this, remember that you'd probably spend longer waiting in line for Starbucks or McDonalds. Remember you can't live on chikn nuggies alone!

Assessment rules:

  1. Take a second to contemplate each question.
  2. Give yourself a number rating between 1 and 5 for each question.
  3. Add up your total and log it below.

Question 1: How are you feeling today?

1 can be the lowest of the low today. That's fine. We all go there sometimes.

2 is you are okay, but just barely.

3 is meh. Not good, and not bad.

4 is pretty darn good! You can keep this up all day.

5 is you are good vibes all the way.

Question 2: Did you drink enough water today?

1 is you didn't have anything.

2 is maybe a glass or two.

3 is almost there, but not quite.

4 is one more glass and you'll be good for the day.

5 is you've hydrated well and then some.

Question 3: Are you feeling anxious?

1 is yes. Everything right now is making you anxious.

2 is able to have calm thoughts, but you might go right back into being anxious.

3 is nothing. No anxiety right now, but it can flaired up.

4 is you are in a good place, but not 100% anxiety free.

5 is worry free and anxiety free.

Question 4: Is there something to be happy about?

1 is a big fat no. There's nothing to be happy about today.

2 is you've smiled at least. A genuine smile for a fleeting moment.

3 is yeah. You are content and happy, but it can change.

4 is almost there. Smiles and love can be felt all around if you just look.

5 is jolly and jovial. Everything is joyous! There are plenty of things to be thankful for!

Question 5: How angry have you been?

1 is very irritable. Everything seems to make you upset or angry. You've yelled or screamed today.

2 is mostly annoyed. Not constantly angry, but you can be easily upset.

3 is neither here nor there. Anger is a fleeting feeling.

4 is content. You can get mad, but not with out reason.

5 is very zen. No anger here!



Time to Score!

Deets. We need the deets.

Remember that this your mood memo. It is a way for you to jot down what you are feeling in this moment. You don't have to think about if it is right or if it is wrong. Feelings are just that. Feelings. The only wrong here is if you leave it blank.

The blurb can be a title or a short snippet of what best describes today.

The stage is set. It is all yours. Take it away!
